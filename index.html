Milestones

Professional Journey

Serious Fraud Investigation Office

Ministry of Corporate Affairs
Legal Associate

My professional journey commenced with the Serious Fraud Investigation Office (SFIO), where I served as a Legal Associate. During this tenure, I gained valuable exposure to large-scale financial and corporate fraud investigations, working closely with Public Prosecutors and Central Government Counsels in trial and appellate litigation. I was entrusted with matters involving complex commercial transactions, regulatory violations, and white-collar criminal allegations before the Hon’ble Bombay High Court, City Civil Courts, Metropolitan Magistrate Courts, and the National Company Law Tribunal. This role not only strengthened my litigation capabilities but also cultivated a strong understanding of financial documentation, business structures, and investigative processes surrounding economic offences.

Enforcement Directorate

Ministry of Finance
Legal Consultant

Thereafter, I joined the Enforcement Directorate as a Legal Consultant. This engagement significantly expanded my expertise in matters involving money laundering, foreign exchange violations, and national economic security. I assisted the Directorate in legal drafting, response to complaints, data analysis, and court-related compliance while working directly with investigating teams and Senior Government Counsels. I appeared and assisted in proceedings before the Hon’ble Gujarat High Court, PMLA Special Court, the Adjudicating Authority, Appellate Tribunal, and various other judicial forums. My responsibilities required meticulous attention to financial evidence, statutory interpretation, and strategic litigation support, deepening my proficiency in financial regulatory law.

Office of the Addl. Solicitor General

Union of India
Counsel Assisting

Subsequently, I had the privilege of joining the Office of the Additional Solicitor General of India, where I assisted in representation of the Union of India before the Hon’ble Bombay High Court. In this role, I contributed to substantial matters concerning constitutional challenges, administrative decisions, and key governmental interests. My work involved preparing pleadings, conducting extensive legal research, and coordinating with central authorities to defend the Government’s legal stand. The courtroom exposure and procedural responsibilities entrusted to me during this time further refined my advocacy, drafting precision, and ability to address high-stakes litigation with clarity and professionalism.

Independent Legal Practice

Present
Advocate High Court

Presently, as an independent practising Advocate before the Hon’ble Bombay High Court and various other courts in Maharashtra and Gujarat, I advise and represent clients in criminal and civil matters relating to financial disputes, commercial conflicts, and economic offences. My practice focuses on litigation involving matters of monetary significance, business liabilities, and regulatory concerns, drawing upon the institutional experience gained from my prior appointments. I actively appear before trial courts, appellate courts, and statutory forums, including NCLT and enforcement tribunals. I continue to remain committed to strategic advocacy, ethical representation, and delivering effective legal solutions to individuals, businesses, and government stakeholders.
